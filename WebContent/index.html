Welcome to Atlas.

the world's first timestamp-based listening service

Whether it's the beat drop at 0:48, the horns at 1:07, or the bassline at 0:08, we all listen to and love songs for some reason or, more specifically, some moment.

At Atlas, we live in that moment - also known as a cherry.

Cherries are those times in songs that give you goosebumps, pump your adrenaline, make your hairs stand on end, or all of the above.

By building a network for people to share those cherries with each other, music discovery and enjoyment are made easier.

Because nobody has time to sit through recommendations.

So don't. Let us do the work for you. All you have to do is sit back, and listen.
